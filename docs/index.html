Growth Funnel & Retention Analysis (E-commerce Case Study)

Oct–Nov 2019 • DuckDB + Parquet • Funnel • Retention Cohorts • Revenue & Observed LTV

Business Summary: This analysis investigates why revenue grew month-over-month and which levers drove performance. The focus is funnel efficiency, pricing behavior, and retention dynamics to simulate how a Growth Analytics team supports product and marketing decisions.

  • View→Cart doubled in November, driven by premium categories
  • Revenue grew MoM and is dominated by high-priced items
  • Strong early repeat behavior, with a sharp drop after week 2
  • Observed LTV varies by cohort; recent cohorts show lower early value

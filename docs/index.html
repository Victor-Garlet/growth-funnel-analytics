Growth Funnel & Retention Analysis (E-commerce Case Study)

Oct-Nov 2019 | DuckDB + Parquet | Funnel | Retention Cohorts | Revenue & Observed LTV

Business Summary: This case study explains what drove revenue growth between October and November 2019 and where conversion quality weakened. The focus is funnel efficiency, cohort retention, and monetization mix to support product, lifecycle, and merchandising decisions.

  • View-to-cart rose from 11.2% to 22.4%, while cart-to-purchase dropped from 60.2% to 48.4%
  • Revenue grew +19.7% MoM, with 95.2% of November revenue concentrated in high-price items
  • Retention decays early: average repeat falls from 13.3% (week 1) to 10.2% (week 2)
  • New cohorts are less valuable early: observed LTV declines from $1,449 to $394 in the latest cohort
